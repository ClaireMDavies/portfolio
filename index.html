About Me

A mother of 4, who has spent most of the last 20 years as a stay at home mum. Having trained as a social worker before having a family, my workplace skills are rather outdated. I have kept myself busy during my time at home with hobbies such as an allotment, teaching myself to spin, knit, crochet, felt and weave, and have run a small online craft business

My other interests include British Sign Language which I have spent a number of years learning. Along with environmental issues, where I am currently co-ordinator for our local Green Party
